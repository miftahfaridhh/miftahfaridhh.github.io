AI & Deep Learning Research 2 projects
Deep Learning / Energy Forecasting / Published

PV Solar Power Forecasting API

Next-day photovoltaic solar power prediction across 10 South Korean sites. Flask REST API with 6 deep learning architectures trained on Korea Meteorological Administration weather data.

Models: BiLSTM, Stacked BiLSTM, LSTM, ConvLSTM, RNN — trained on 12 weather features (soil temp, solar radiation, cloud cover, etc.) with 24-hour sliding window producing 24-hour forecasts. MinMax normalization with tanh activation.

Result: BiLSTM variants achieved lowest prediction error. Automated daily predictions at 10:00 and 16:00 KST. Published in Sensors 2023 (MDPI) and ICUFN 2023.

Deep Learning / Time-Series

Smart Home Energy Forecasting

Comparative study: ARIMA/SARIMA/SARIMAX vs LSTM/BiLSTM/GRU/RNN on 503,910 smart meter records with weather features.

Key finding: LSTM Multivariate achieves MSE=0.022, MAE=0.110 — significantly outperforming classical methods. Extended to 17 South Korean regions with BiLSTM achieving R²=0.9549 on Busan data.
